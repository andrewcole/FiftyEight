Kirov Vodka

Vodka is currently the largest growing white spirit category in Australia and Kirov is one of the country's leading vodka brands, especially in Australia's tropics. Kirov forms the popular spirit base for a large range of cocktails and is a very clean, neutral vodka with quality spirit characters.

As a high quality vodka, it is often enjoyed neat, from the freezer, in a shot glass.

Also available are two new flavoured vodkas - Kirov Raspberry and Kirov Lemon/Lime
